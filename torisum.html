About Dantewada

Dantewada is a district rich in culture, history, and natural beauty in the state of Chhattisgarh, India. It is known for its tribal heritage, lush forests, and religious sites. The district attracts tourists and devotees from across the country, offering a glimpse into traditional lifestyles and ancient temples.
